Today's Featured Recipe:

5-Min Salmon Salad

by Angel and Emilia

Ingredients:

Canned salmon

Mayonnaise

Celery

Onion

Dill

Black pepper

Lemon juice

Preparation

Step 1: Drain the salmon and chop the onions and celery.

Step 2: In a bowl, combine the ingredients and mix well.

Step 3: Add extra mayo to reach your desired creaminess.

Step 4: Serve however you please.
